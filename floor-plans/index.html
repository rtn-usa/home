Apartment Floor Plan Layouts

3 layouts for a 2BR angular apartment • Feng shui guided • Dog-friendly

1. Balanced Harmony

Classic

One desk per bedroom. Carin sofa centered in living room facing TV. Full pull-out sofa/bed in 2nd bedroom for guests. Round dining table seats 4.

Desk in each bedroom Pull-out sofa bed Round dining table Dog bed by sofa

2. Open Flow

Studio Office

Both desks in 2nd bedroom for a dedicated two-person office. Murphy bed for guests. Carin sofa as living room divider. Bar-height dining along kitchen wall.

Dual-desk office Murphy bed Bar-height dining Sofa as divider

3. Cozy Zones

Multi-functional

Carin sofa in 2nd bedroom as TV lounge + pull-out guest bed. Desk in master + desk in living. Bench seating, poufs, drop-leaf table. Maximum dog-friendly.

Den/lounge room Drop-leaf dining Built-in bench 2 dog zones

Shared Requirements Across All Layouts

  • Beds: 1 queen (master), 1 full guest (pull-out or murphy)
  • Desks: 2 workspaces
  • Sofa: Joybird Carin couch
  • Storage: IKEA PAX at entry + IKEA PAX in 2nd bedroom + additional shelves
  • Dining: Seating for 4
  • Dogs: Dedicated lounge areas
  • Feng Shui: Command position beds, clear chi flow from entry, no furniture blocking doors, five element balance
