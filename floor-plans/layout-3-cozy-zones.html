Layout 3: Cozy Zones - Design Notes

  • 2nd Bedroom (Den/Lounge/Guest): The Joybird Carin sofa moves here as the centerpiece - pulls out to a full guest bed. TV and media console on the opposite wall. Bookshelf and side table complete a cozy reading/TV nook. Dog lounge area between sofa and TV. IKEA PAX along the right wall.
  • Master Bedroom: Queen bed in command position (head against left wall, sightline to door). Nightstands on each side. Desk 1 in the far corner for a private workspace, chair faces room. Dog bed near the door so pups can choose between rooms.
  • Living / Dining: A built-in bench seat along the angled left wall provides seating that doubles for dining, lounging, or laptop work. Drop-leaf dining table near the kitchen (expands for meals, folds for space). Two floor poufs add flexible seating. Desk 2 tucked beside the dining area as a compact work nook. Floating shelves on the dividing wall for display/storage.
  • Multi-functional Design: Bench + poufs + drop-leaf table reconfigure for: dinner party (table open, all seats), work mode (desk + bench with laptop), movie night (poufs to den), dog hangout (dogs on bench or poufs).
  • Dog-Friendly: Two dedicated dog zones (den + master), bench seat they can hop on, open floor paths between rooms.
  • Feng Shui Five Elements: Wood (plants in corners), Water (mirror at entry), Earth (natural textile poufs), Fire (warm-toned lighting), Metal (floating shelves, desk frames).
