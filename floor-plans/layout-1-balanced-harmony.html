Layout 1: Balanced Harmony - Design Notes

  • Master Bedroom: Queen bed in feng shui command position - head against the left (exterior) wall with clear sightline to the door. Two nightstands for balance. Desk 1 along the right wall near the door, chair facing into the room (not wall-facing for better energy).
  • 2nd Bedroom (Guest Room + Office): Full-size pull-out sofa/bed against the top wall in command position - doubles as seating when not in use. IKEA PAX wardrobe along the right wall near kitchen divider. Desk 2 along the bottom wall with a bookshelf. When no guests, it's a home office.
  • Living / Dining: Joybird Carin sofa against the angled left wall, facing the TV wall-mounted on the living/master dividing wall. Round dining table (seats 4) near the kitchen opening for easy serving. Pouf between sofa and dining for flexible seating.
  • Dog Space: Dog bed beside the sofa, near you but out of the main traffic path from entry to kitchen.
  • Entry: IKEA PAX in the entry hallway for coats, shoes, and keys.
  • Feng Shui: Clear chi flow from entry through living. Beds in command positions (see door, not directly in line with it). Round dining table promotes harmony. No furniture blocks doorways.
