Layout 1: Balanced Harmony - Design Notes

  • Master Bedroom: Queen bed in feng shui command position - head against the solid south wall, with a clear sightline to the door. Two nightstands for balance. Desk 1 along the east wall, near natural light, with chair facing the room (not wall-facing for energy flow).
  • 2nd Bedroom / Guest Room: Full-size pull-out sofa/bed against the west wall in command position. IKEA PAX wardrobe along the upper wall for storage. Desk 2 along the lower wall with a bookshelf nearby. When guests aren't visiting, the room functions as a home office.
  • Living / Dining: The Joybird Carin sofa is centered facing the south wall with TV mounted above a media console. Round dining table (seats 4) near the kitchen pass-through for easy serving. A pouf/ottoman between sofa and dining provides flexible seating for work, lounging, or extra dining.
  • Dog Space: A dedicated dog bed/lounging area beside the sofa where they can be near you but out of the traffic flow.
  • Entry: IKEA PAX in the entryway hallway for coats, shoes, and keys - creating an organized first impression.
  • Feng Shui Principles: Clear chi flow path from entry through living space. No furniture blocking doorways. Beds in command positions. Round dining table promotes harmony. Plants recommended at the entry and living room corners.
