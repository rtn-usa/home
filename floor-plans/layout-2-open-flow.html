Layout 2: Open Flow - Design Notes

  • 2nd Bedroom (Dedicated Office + Guest): Both desks side-by-side along the bottom wall facing inward - a proper two-person office. Murphy bed folds down from the left wall when guests visit. IKEA PAX along the top wall. Filing cabinet for office storage.
  • Master Bedroom: Queen bed centered with head against the bottom wall in command position. Nightstands on each side. Dresser near the door. Dog bed in the far corner for a cozy retreat.
  • Living / Dining: Carin sofa faces the TV wall-mounted on the bedroom/living divider wall. Console table behind the sofa creates a visual boundary. Bar-height dining table along the kitchen counter with 4 stools - great for casual meals and doubles as extra workspace. Accent chair adds flexible seating.
  • Dog Space: Two spots - a primary lounge beside the sofa in living, and a bed in the master bedroom corner.
  • Feng Shui: Sofa has solid backing (console table + wall). Clear sightlines from entry. Murphy bed keeps office energy clear when not in guest mode. Bar table creates a gathering point without blocking energy flow.
